The Cipher

The Rail Fence Cipher is a transposition cipher.
It rearranges the plaintext letters by drawing them in a way that they form a shape of the rails of an imaginary fence.
The Rail Fence Cipher was invented in ancient times.

It was used by the Greeks, who created a special tool, called scytale, to make message encryption and decryption easier.
Currently, it is usually used with a piece of paper.

The letters are arranged in a way which is similar to the shape of the top edge of the rail fence.
