Caesar's Cipher


Caesars Cipher, also known as Caesars Shift, Shift Cipher or Caesars Code,
is one of the most well known encrytion methods. It is a "plain text" substituion Cipher
it works by shifting letters along the alphabet by a user inputted step amount.

It is unknown how successfull the cipher actually was at the time however currently the cipher has no communication security

The cipher is named such as it was recorded first being used by the Roman Empiror Julius Caesar.

The cipher has been used to create children's toy made out of 2 rotating disks to encypt and decrypt the code

Although a basic cipher it was still being used in 1915 by the Russian army.
